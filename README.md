# Fatal Tarot

   Constructor Press Play Game Jam   
   Built in 48 hours at Constructor University Bremen

## What is Fatal Tarot

FATAL TAROT is a minimalist tabletop strategy game that reimagines competitive card duels through the lens of fate and chance. Instead of brute force or complex combos, victory is earned by the weight of tarot cards placed upon the table of fate.

Every match is a short ritual of tension between you and a calm, unreadable AI opponent. On the surface, the rules are simple. But in practice, nothing in a reading of fate is ever as fixed as it first appears.

The game unfolds in a dim, symbolic space where every card feels like it already had an outcome before it was played. Or at least, it should have.
